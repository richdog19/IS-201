Overcoming Poor Health Habits


Leading Causes of Poor Health

  1. Consuming Materials Not Intended for Human Consumption
    • Ding Dongs
    • Candy
    • Tide Pods
  2. Life is Too Physically Easy
    • Lack of physical acitivity in most well-paid jobs
    • No real need to walk more than maybe a half mile a day
    • No real danger of starving to death
  3. Passive Hobbies
    • Watching sports instead of playing them
    • Virtual Interaction replacing physical interaction
    • Increasing cultural acceptance of unhealthy lifestyle decisions
